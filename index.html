Chimera Arts & Maker Space

Chimera is Sonoma County's first community arts and maker space.
We're a diverse group of people who love making things and sharing what we know.

Chimera Art Space is a community-driven 501(c)3 non-profit workspace where engineers, artists, students, and hobbyists come to make prototypes, art, and other creative projects.
